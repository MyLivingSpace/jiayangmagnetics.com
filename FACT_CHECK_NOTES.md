# Fact-check notes — Jiayang Magnetics international site

This file lists **unclear, conflicting, or externally unverified** facts encountered when comparing the international site (`jiayangmagnetics.com` codebase) with the legacy English site (`http://www.hzjy-cnt.com/en`) and public materials. **Nothing here authorizes deleting on-site claims without Jiayang legal/marketing sign-off.**

## 1. Core manufacturing scope (material vs. editorial correction)

- The **legacy English homepage** (fetched 2026) states that Jiayang “independently develops and manufactures nanocrystalline amorphous alloy ribbons and nanocrystalline cores in-house” and describes a “vertically integrated, end-to-end manufacturing system.”
- The **international site** intentionally uses **narrower, credibility-first wording**: selected nanocrystalline core capabilities, qualified core sourcing, core material selection and validation, and emphasis on **in-house transformer/inductor winding, assembly, encapsulation, and testing**.
- **Action:** Treat the legacy EN copy as **marketing language that may overstate** what should be claimed on the export-facing site until Jiayang confirms an approved technical and legal position. Reconcile Chinese vs. English domestic pages the same way.

## 2. “National High-Tech Enterprise” vs. provincial recognition

- Legacy EN site: “recognized as a **National** High-Tech Enterprise.”
- International site milestones: **“Guangdong Provincial High-Tech Enterprise”** (2016, 2023) — not “National.”
- **Open item:** Confirm with Jiayang whether any **national** high-tech enterprise certificate exists and, if so, the **exact certificate title and issuing authority** before using “National” on the international site.

## 3. Legal / brand English name on legacy site

- Legacy EN header uses **“Huizhou Jiayang Electronics Core New-tech Co.,Ltd”** (variant spelling/structure).
- International site standard: **Huizhou Jiayang Electronic Technology Co., Ltd.** (`惠州佳扬电子科技有限公司`) as legal entity; **Jiayang Magnetics** as international brand only.
- **Note:** Align printed English legal name on contracts and footers with the company chop — do not infer from the old website header alone.

## 4. Hubei base naming (Wuhan Chenyang vs. Wuhan/Xiantao)

- Site content uses **“Wuhan/Xiantao (Hubei)”** in the company facilities string and timeline entries referencing **“Wuhan Chenyang.”**
- Legacy EN content refers generically to **two bases in Guangdong and Hubei** without resolving Chenyang vs. Xiantao naming for international readers.
- **Open item:** Confirm the **customer-facing English name** of the Hubei operating entity and location(s) for website and imprint consistency.

## 5. Workforce education statistic (legacy site only)

- Legacy EN “Strong R&D” block: **310 employees hold associate degrees or higher (21% of workforce)**.
- This statistic is **not reproduced** on the international site (avoid carrying unverified numbers).
- **Open item:** If Jiayang wants it published, obtain the **source document and year** for the figure.

## 6. Customer list completeness

- Legacy EN homepage lists example customers (e.g. Midea, Xiaomi, Haier, Huawei, Gree, TCL).
- International site uses a **curated, grouped list** with a disclosure that references are **public / cleared** and not exhaustive.
- **Open item:** Periodically verify names still appear on Jiayang’s **authorized** public references and that export marketing rights exist for each logo asset.

## 7. Registered capital, scale, certifications (partially aligned)

- **Founded May 2002**, **RMB 60M registered capital**, **~1,500 employees**, **~112 R&D engineers**, **60,000 ㎡**, **two bases (Guangdong / Hubei)**, **ISO 9001 / ISO 14001 / IATF 16949**, **UL / CQC product certifications**, and **address / phone numbers** are **consistent** between legacy EN summary blocks and the international site’s primary facts.
- **RoHS:** Legacy text does not use the same compliance framing; international site uses **“RoHS-compliant”** (not “RoHS certified”) — confirm with compliance team.

## 8. Amorphous material datasheet row “Ribbon thickness”

- Amorphous grade comparison tables migrated from the legacy catalog include a **material property row** labeled “Ribbon thickness (µm).” This describes **amorphous alloy strip thickness as a material parameter**, not a claim that Jiayang manufactures all ribbon in-house.
- If this row is misread by non-technical readers, add a **short footnote on the product page** (copy approved by engineering).
