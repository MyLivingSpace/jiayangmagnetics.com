Site logo upload path
=====================

Place your logo file here (same basename):
  website/public/images/brand/jiayang-magnetics-logo.svg
or
  website/public/images/brand/jiayang-magnetics-logo.png
or
  website/public/images/brand/jiayang-magnetics-logo.jpg
or
  website/public/images/brand/jiayang-magnetics-logo.jpeg
or
  website/public/images/brand/jiayang-magnetics-logo.webp

Current behavior:
- Header tries formats in this order:
  .svg -> .png -> .jpg -> .jpeg -> .webp
- If missing, Header falls back to the "JM" logo chip.

Tip:
- Preferred format: SVG with transparent background.
- Keep the artwork centered and square-friendly for best results in the 36x36 logo slot.
