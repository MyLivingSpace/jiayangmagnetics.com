Certificates upload folder (Resources page downloads)
=====================================================
Place certificate PDFs in this folder:
  website/public/resources/certificates/

Use these exact filenames (case-sensitive):
  iso-9001-certificate.pdf
  iso-14001-certificate.pdf
  iatf-16949-certificate.pdf
  ul-cqc-certificates.pdf

Notes:
- These files are linked in /resources as direct downloads.
- Patent certificates remain "Available on request" and are not in this download set.
